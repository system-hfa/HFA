These events can be explained by the combination of these
  associated factors:

 The feedback mechanisms of all of those invloved
 An absence of training, at high altitude, in manual aeroplane

  handling and the procedure relating to speed indication
  anomalies
 Task-sharing weakened by the startle effect and by

  incomprehension of the situation
 Lack of a clear display in the cockpit of the airspeed

  inconsistencies identified by the computers
 The crew not taking into account the stall warning
