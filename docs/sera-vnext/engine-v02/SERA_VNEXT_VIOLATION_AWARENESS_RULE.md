# SERA vNext Violation Awareness Rule

O-B and O-C are not emitted from mere continuation, poor result, pressure, or post-escape consequence.

Required evidence:
1. the relevant rule/procedure was known;
2. the actor was aware of the situation/rule at the escape point;
3. the deviation was conscious;
4. the routine or exceptional subtype is positively supported.

Boundary outcomes:
- explicit awareness plus routine deviation can reach O-B;
- explicit awareness plus exceptional deviation can reach O-C;
- known rule without situation awareness abstains;
- unknown rule abstains from O-B/O-C;
- continuation without intent/awareness abstains from O-B/O-C.

Tests:
- `V02-LEAF-O-B-PT`
- `V02-LEAF-O-C-EN`
- `V02-INDEP-PT-VIOL-NO-AWARE`
- `V02-INDEP-EN-VIOL-NO-AWARE`
