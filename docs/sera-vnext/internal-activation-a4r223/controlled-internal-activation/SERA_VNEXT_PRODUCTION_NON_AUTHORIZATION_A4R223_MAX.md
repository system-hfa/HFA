# Production Non-Authorization A4R223-MAX

A4R223 does not authorize:
- public or production activation;
- common-user access;
- classification or engine execution;
- upload, write, database mutation, or migration;
- P/O/A, selectedCode, releasedCode, finalConclusion, CLASSIFIED, or READY output;
- downstream, HFACS, Risk/ERC, ARMS/ERC, or recommendations.

Only local controlled activation was executed. A separate explicit human authorization and a real enterprise-admin staging smoke are required before any broader operational step.
