# SERA vNext Reviewer Checklist

## Escape Point

- Does the statement identify the first useful point to leave or restore the safe operation?
- Is it earlier than the consequence?
- Is it not merely a precondition?
- Is the direct actor supported by the narrative?
- Is the evidence contemporaneous with the escape point?

## Perception

- What did the direct actor perceive or fail to perceive at the escape point?
- Is awareness evidence explicit enough?
- Is hindsight being used?
- Is the correct answer more evidence needed?

## Objective

- Was the objective assessed at the escape point?
- Is any confidence-style objective supported by awareness evidence?
- Is later continuation being used incorrectly as objective evidence?

## Action

- What action or omission occurred at the escape point?
- Is the action coherent with the actor's perception?
- Is any confidence-style action supported by feedback or verification evidence?

## Preconditions

- Are preconditions separated from the escape point?
- Is each precondition supported by evidence?
- Is each precondition framed as context or candidate contribution instead of final cause?

## Human Decision

- `ACCEPT_AS_WORKING_HYPOTHESIS` when the hypothesis is useful and evidence-supported.
- `REJECT_WORKING_HYPOTHESIS` when the hypothesis is not defensible.
- `REQUIRES_MORE_EVIDENCE` when facts are insufficient.
- `RETURN_FOR_REANALYSIS` when the narrative was changed or new evidence was added.
