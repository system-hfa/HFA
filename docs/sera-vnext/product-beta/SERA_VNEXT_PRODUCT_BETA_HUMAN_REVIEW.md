# SERA vNext Product Beta Human Review

Allowed decisions:

- `ACCEPT_AS_WORKING_HYPOTHESIS`
- `REJECT_WORKING_HYPOTHESIS`
- `REQUIRES_MORE_EVIDENCE`
- `RETURN_FOR_REANALYSIS`

Forbidden decisions:

- `FINAL_CLASSIFICATION`
- `RELEASED`
- `READY`
- `CLASSIFIED`

Human review records are separate from machine output. They do not mutate the engine output and do not release SERA codes.
